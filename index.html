projects

vibes & vino

vibes & vino: DIY music showcase providing a platform for Stanford musicians to perform, collaborate, and experiment, as well as a temporary space for students in general to collectively envision a campus music scene.

Gray Area: spent the summer of 2019 as a creative development and production intern for Gray Area Foundation for the Arts in San Francisco.

ArtX: campus org that is bridging the divide between art and technology on campus. 2019-20 events coordinator, spearheading NEW DIMENSIONALITY, an experimental live showcase.

braden storytelling grant: awarded funding to travel to Asheville, North Carolina to produce an audio story about my experiences with DIY music communities and combatting the idea of “the hey day.” listen here.

soft-boiled on kzsu: host a weekly show on kzsu stanford 90.1fm called ’soft-boiled’; spinning music that seems tough on the outside, but makes you feel gooey on the inside.

art

INVERTIGO (2019): “I don’t know which way is up anymore.” a short film created by Hannah Scott and Dexter Simpson, starring Jake Goldstein, with an original score composed and performed by Sophie Boyd-Fliegel.

Personal Space (2019):an experimental short that aims to represent the multiple planes of existence on which we operate at any given moment. Inspirations for this piece include Maya Deren, Andrei Tarkovsky, and Georges Perec.
